The fresh salmon are caught right here from the Puget Sound.
They are finely ground that you won't detect the smell,
but still get the health benefits of proteins, Omega-3 Fatty Acids, Vitamin B and Minerals from salmons.
Pat bakes her salmon cookies in the early dawn everyday with farm fresh, finest, and organic ingredients.
Bake with you in mind!
